Research Opportunities

We offer exciting thesis opportunities for students interested in satellite technology, machine learning, and marine monitoring

Processing and Analysis for SAR and Optical Satellite Images

Machine Learning Remote-Sensing Geophysics

Develop advanced algorithms for processing and analyzing synthetic aperture radar (SAR) and optical satellite imagery to extract meaningful insights for marine monitoring applications. This research involves working with cutting-edge image processing techniques, deep learning models, and real-world satellite data to detect and classify maritime activities.

Requirements:
  • Background in Computer Science, Geophysics, or related field
  • Knowledge of image processing and machine learning
  • Programming experience in Python or MATLAB
  • Interest in satellite remote sensing
Apply Now

Numerical Methods for Feature Extraction

Mathematics Statistics

Research and implement advanced numerical methods and statistical approaches to improve detection accuracy and feature extraction from complex satellite data sets. Focus on developing robust algorithms that can handle noise, uncertainty, and large-scale data processing challenges.

Requirements:
  • Strong background in Mathematics or Statistics
  • Experience with numerical analysis and optimization
  • Programming skills in Python, R, or C++
  • Knowledge of statistical modeling
Apply Now

System Integration of Different Services

Computer Science Software Engineering

Design and develop integrated systems that combine multiple services and technologies into cohesive solutions for satellite-based monitoring and analytics platforms. Work on microservices architecture, API design, and cloud-based deployment strategies.

Requirements:
  • Computer Science or Software Engineering background
  • Experience with system architecture and API design
  • Knowledge of cloud platforms and containerization
  • Familiarity with microservices and distributed systems
Apply Now

What We Offer

  • Access to real satellite data and cutting-edge technology
  • Mentorship from experienced researchers and industry professionals
  • Potential for publication and conference presentations
  • Networking opportunities in the space technology industry
  • Possible pathway to full-time employment

How to Apply

To apply for a thesis opportunity, please send us your CV, a brief cover letter explaining your interest, and any relevant academic transcripts. We encourage applications from students at all levels (Bachelor's, Master's, and PhD).
